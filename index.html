About Us

Built on a
Legacy of Hard Work

Prohaul delivers dependable, nationwide haulage solutions in Jamaica. Founded by the late Douglas Freeman, Prohaul is built on a legacy of hard work, integrity, and a deep understanding of the haulage industry. The knowledge and values he instilled continue to guide our operations today.

We simplify logistics for our clients by eliminating the hassle of complex communication channels and multiple contractors, offering a seamless, all-in-one haulage service. While we operate our own fleet, we are not limited by it. Prohaul partners with like-minded, value-aligned operators across the industry, ensuring we always have the capacity and flexibility to tackle any project, no matter the scale.

When challenges arise, we solve them. When deadlines loom, we meet them. Our clients trust us not only to move their materials but to move their projects forward.
